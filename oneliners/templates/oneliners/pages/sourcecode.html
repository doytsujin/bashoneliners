The site is far from what we want it to be. Mainly we need more Django developers. But also reviewers of the contributed one-liners, to improve or remove those that are sub-optimal. At the minimum it would help a lot if you could spread the word and talk about this site on Twitter, Google+, Facebook, Reddit or other social media.

This page should give you all the links, resources and contact info that you might need to participate in this project. Do let us know if we missed something.

Right now the site is still pretty far from what we want it to be. Our next bigger goals:

  • Perfect one-liners following today's best-practices: Expert users should be able to edit and improve poorly written one-liners. The site needs peer-review and collaborative editing features, similar to stackoverflow.com, but a bit differently. On Stack Overflow, answers do not collaborate with each other, and sometimes the accepted answer is not optimal. Sometimes the best answer would be a combination of multiple answers. Our goal here is to encourage users to optimize the heck out of every single one-liner until it is perfect.
  • Gamification: Gamification features could be important to attract and motivate contributions. Designing the system is a very complex subject, it takes much more than just coding.

To make all these things happen we need more Django coders, espeically with gamification experience.
